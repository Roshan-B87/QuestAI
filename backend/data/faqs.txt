Q: What is the last date for fee payment?
A: The last date for fee payment is 31st March 2025. A late fee of Rs 500 will be charged after this date.

Q: How can I apply for a scholarship?
A: Students can apply for scholarships via the college portal under the Finance section. Eligibility: 60% and above in previous year.

Q: When are the exams?
A: End semester exams are scheduled from 15th April to 30th April 2025. Timetable will be available on the notice board.

Q: How do I get a hostel room?
A: Hostel allotment is done at the beginning of the academic year. Contact the hostel warden office for current availability.

Q: What are the library timings?
A: The library is open Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM. Sunday is closed.

Q: How do I get my result?
A: Results are published on the college website within 30 days of the exam. Login with your roll number to view your result.
